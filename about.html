Development Story

This website was developed through an interactive conversation with Claude AI in Cursor IDE. The entire process, from initial concept to final implementation, took approximately 45 minutes.

The development workflow involved:

  • Initial scaffolding of the site structure based on requirements
  • Iterative refinement of content and styling through natural language conversation
  • Real-time collaboration where I guided the AI to make specific adjustments to design and functionality
  • Rapid prototyping and implementation of design changes

This approach demonstrated the efficiency of AI-assisted development while maintaining full creative control over the final product. The result is a professional, responsive website that was developed in a fraction of the time traditional development would require.
